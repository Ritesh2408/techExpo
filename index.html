About


  • MindSpark’21, the National Level Technical Festival of College of Engineering Pune is in its 15th edition this year. It organizes a Technical Exhibition every year which serves as a platform for start-ups and companies to showcase their innovative research and technology

  • Technical Exhibition of Mindspark’21 will continue its role of providing start-ups and companies with the opportunity to spread their ideas and build their network. Last year, we witnessed a footfall of 22,000 consisting of engineering students, CoEP alumni, industry professionals and technology enthusiasts.

  • This year, the Exhibition will witness a wide array of innovations and technologies, TechTalks by Entrepreneurs, Industry Experts and Technologists, sharing their experience and introducing innovation to the audience in the fields of Automation, Humanoid Robots and Defence.

  • This year, we also look forward to expand our platform to the Technical Projects in the fields of Bio-engineering, Automation and Humanoid robots. This will not only provide participants with the opportunity to interact with our sponsors who are some of the industry leaders but also help them get visibility and valuable feedback.
